The Vision Of the Faculty

“to extend its’ facilities and services to develop as a premier centre for higher education and advanced research in the field of technology, thus enabling students following the degree programme to obtain a sound knowledge and acquire multi-disciplinary skills”.

The Mission Of the Faculty

“to search for and disseminate knowledge in the areas of learning that will make a useful contribution to the development of critical manpower requirements of the nation; contribute to education, science and technology and socio economic upliftment of the communities of the nation.”
